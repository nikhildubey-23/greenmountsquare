PROJECT NAME: GREENMOUNT SQUARE

PROJECT TYPE:
Commercial Retail & Business Complex

LOCATION:
GreenMount Square, Opposite Rama Life City, Mungeli Road, Sakri, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, India.

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
GreenMount Square is a premium commercial development strategically located in Sakri, Bilaspur. The project is designed to maximize business visibility through its prominent corner architecture, high-exposure frontage, and modern commercial design. The development is surrounded by strong social infrastructure and approximately 5,000 nearby residential families, creating consistent customer footfall and business opportunities.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS:
• Prime commercial location in Sakri, Bilaspur
• Corner plot with high visibility
• Modern architectural façade
• Strong residential catchment of approximately 5,000 families
• Close proximity to Jain International School
• Excellent connectivity to Sakri market and Uslapur corridor
• Suitable for retail shops, showrooms, offices, service businesses, and commercial investment

LOCATION ADVANTAGES:
• Situated in one of Bilaspur's well-known and centrally connected neighborhoods
• Near Jain International School
• Connected through the Uslapur railway development corridor
• Easy access to major residential and commercial zones
• Located on Mungeli Road, a growing commercial stretch
