GreenMount Square is a premium commercial development strategically located in Sakri, Bilaspur. The project is designed to maximize business visibility through its prominent corner architecture, high-exposure frontage, and modern commercial design.

The development is surrounded by strong social infrastructure and approximately 5,000 nearby residential families, creating consistent customer footfall and business opportunities.
